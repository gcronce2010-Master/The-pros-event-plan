# **App Name**: AI Party

## Core Features:

- Event Creation & Management: Users can create and manage event details such as date, time, location, theme, and descriptions.
- Guest Invitation & RSVP: Invite guests via email or shareable link and track their RSVP status, including custom questions for dietary needs or preferences.
- Real-time Two-Way Chat: Enable instant communication between organizers and invited guests within each event, supporting planning discussions and real-time updates.
- AI Party Theme Brainstormer: A generative AI tool that suggests creative party themes, decor ideas, and activities based on occasion, guest count, and preferred vibe.
- Task & To-Do Lists: Organizers can create and assign tasks related to event planning, with due dates and completion tracking.
- Guest List Messaging: Send broadcast messages to all guests or individual messages for private conversations outside the main event chat.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen primary color is a vibrant yet approachable Coral Orange (#E65C33), representing warmth, energy, and sociability, suitable for primary actions and highlights.
- A soft, inviting Light Warm Off-White (#F7F0EE) serves as the background, providing a clean and contemporary canvas that complements the vibrant primary.
- An accent color of Deep Raspberry/Plum (#AD2E55) offers a sophisticated contrast, ideal for secondary calls to action or emphasizing critical information without clashing.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, slightly technical, and impactful appearance that grabs attention.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability and neutral, objective character, perfect for chat messages, event details, and other textual content.
- Use line-art style icons that are clear, friendly, and visually consistent, supporting intuitive navigation and feature recognition.
- Subtle and purposeful micro-interactions, such as fades for new messages or slight expands on button hovers, will enhance the user experience without distraction.
